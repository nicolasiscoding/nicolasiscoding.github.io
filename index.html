About me

I am currently a 3rd year junior at the University of Florida, but before that, I attended the College Academy @ Broward College.

Last spring, I had the wonderful opportunity to intern at Ultimate Software as a Software Test Engineer. I happened to win 2nd place in their 48 hours competition for the audience's choice category with my databse diff. tool called 'Grazie'.

I learned more than I could have imagined and I am extremely thankful for this awesome opportunity.

In terms of perspective opportunities, I am available for Summer 2016. I would love to work in Software Sales, and I am also up to solving some problems with computer vision. As always, I am open for other opportunities as well!
